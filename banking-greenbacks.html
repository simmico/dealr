Nedbank Greenbacks.

Simpler than most SA reward programmes - but the Amex card hiding in your wallet doubles your earn rate on every transaction where it's accepted.

How Greenbacks works

Nedbank Greenbacks is built around passive earning - points accumulate automatically on all eligible card transactions without requiring tier management or monthly behaviour goals. It is the most low-maintenance major banking reward programme in South Africa.

Greenbacks membership costs R35 per month, though it is included free on certain qualifying premium Nedbank accounts. Your Greenbacks never expire, so there is no rush to redeem before a cut-off date.

The value conversion is: 36 Greenbacks = R1.00 when routing your balance to a linked Amex or Mastercard platform. That translates to a roughly 2.8% return on qualifying spend, though the Amex earn rate makes the real-world figure considerably higher on a dual-card setup.

The American Express double-earn rule

This is the most important thing any Nedbank Greenbacks cardholder needs to know. Nedbank issues qualifying accounts with two cards: a Mastercard and an American Express. They link to the same account, but they earn at different rates.

Whenever a merchant accepts American Express, use the Amex card. Every transaction on the Amex triggers a double Greenbacks earn rate compared to the standard Mastercard. The same spend, the same merchant, twice the points - simply by choosing the right card at the terminal.

South African merchants that accept Amex include most major retailers, petrol stations, restaurants, and online shops. The gap in your points balance adds up faster than most people expect over a year of consistent spending.

BP fuel rewards

Nedbank has a dedicated fuel partnership with BP service stations. Swiping your registered Nedbank card at BP earns you a flat 25 cents per litre back in cash - on top of your standard Greenbacks earn rate. If you are a BP regular, this alone is a compelling reason to keep your Nedbank card as your primary payment method at the pump.

Pair this with the Amex card at BP where accepted, and you are stacking both the fuel partner bonus and the higher Amex earn rate simultaneously. Note: the 25c per litre cashback does not apply when paying with the Greenbacks SHOP Card.

Turning points into cash at the ATM

One of Greenbacks' most practical features is the ability to convert your points balance directly to cash and withdraw it at any Nedbank ATM - no voucher codes, no partner portals, no waiting for a statement credit. Cash. In hand. To do this, you use your complimentary Greenbacks SHOP Card - a free American Express chip-and-pin card issued to Greenbacks members.

Alternatively, you can transfer your Greenbacks directly into a Nedbank investment account, including JustInvest, Platinum Invest, MoneyTrader, a Unit Trust, or your MyPocket, allowing your reward balance to compound alongside your savings rather than sitting idle in a loyalty wallet.

For those who prefer simplicity over complexity, this makes Greenbacks one of the most usable reward currencies in South Africa - the exit is straightforward and the value is guaranteed.

Verify and explore

Current Greenbacks earn rates, partner promotions, and conversion options are published on the Nedbank website. Always confirm the current double-earn rate terms on Amex before planning a major spend.
