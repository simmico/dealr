HealthyFood Cashback.

Up to 25% back at Checkers and Woolworths — here's how to actually get it.

The three rules most people miss

You get cash back in Discovery Miles on thousands of healthy grocery products at Checkers and Woolworths. Sounds simple — but the system has a few hidden rules that stop most people from getting the full 25%.

  • Your plan type sets your ceiling. Standard Vitality Health members can earn up to 25% back. If you're on the cheaper Vitality Active plan (R159/mo per adult), you're permanently capped at 10% — no matter how much healthy food you buy.
  • You have to pick a Primary Partner to unlock 25%. Link both Checkers and Woolworths and you'll get 10% cashback at each store. Want 25% at one? You have to log into the Discovery portal and manually designate that store as your Primary Partner. Your other store stays locked at a maximum of 10%. Discovery won't do this automatically.
  • Your ID numbers have to match — exactly. For Woolworths, link your WRewards Card via the Discovery App. For Checkers, link your Xtra Savings card. The South African ID number on your loyalty profile must be identical to the one on your Discovery contract. A single digit difference means zero cashback tracked — not less, just nothing.
