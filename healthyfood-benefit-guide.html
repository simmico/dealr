HealthyFood Cashback.

Up to 25% back at Checkers and Woolworths - here's how to actually get it.

What the HealthyFood benefit actually gives you

Discovery Vitality's HealthyFood benefit gives you up to 25% back in Discovery Miles on qualifying healthy grocery items at Checkers and Woolworths. Qualifying items include vegetables, fruit, wholegrain and high-fibre starchy foods, lean protein, fat-free dairy, legumes, and healthy fats and oils. The rewards are paid as Discovery Miles into the Vitality Mall - at a rate of 10 Discovery Miles for every rand earned - where the main member can redeem them.

The benefit applies to Vitality Health members (18+) and Vitality Active members (18+). Each qualifying adult on the membership - main member, spouse, adult dependant, or child dependant 18 or older - must activate the benefit on their own Vitality profile to earn rewards. Rewards from all family members contribute to the same monthly total, paid into the main member's Discovery Miles balance.

The three rules most people miss

The system has several hidden rules that prevent most members from reaching the full 25%.

  • You must pick a Primary Partner to unlock 25%. Your rewards are split between a primary in-store partner and a primary online partner - chosen separately. Primary partners earn up to 25% back; your non-primary ("secondary") partner earns up to 10% back. If you never designate a primary partner, you receive a lower default rate at both stores. Log into the Discovery app or website, navigate to the HealthyFood benefit, and manually set your primary in-store partner (Checkers or Woolworths Food) and your primary online partner (Checkers Sixty60 or Woolworths Online, Woolies app and Woolies Dash). Discovery will not do this automatically.
  • Your plan type controls the ceiling and how many primaries you can set. Vitality Health members can choose separate primary partners for in-store and online channels - potentially earning 25% at Checkers in-store and 25% at Woolworths Online simultaneously. Vitality Active members follow the same structure - verify the current rules for your specific product in the Discovery portal, as they can differ.
  • Your ID numbers must match exactly on every profile. For Woolworths, you need a Woolworths MyDifference card (available via the Woolies app) linked to your ID. For Checkers, you need a Checkers Xtra Savings card linked to your ID on the Checkers website. The South African ID number on your loyalty card profile must match exactly with the ID on your Discovery contract. A single digit difference means zero cashback tracked - not less, just nothing. You can create a Checkers Xtra Savings card directly during the HealthyFood activation process if you do not have one yet. Verify your ID on the Discovery Update Details page; you will receive an SMS or email confirmation once verified.
  • Primary partner changes are restricted. Once the HealthyFood benefit is activated, only the main member can change primary partners - and only once every rolling 12-month period from the date of activation. Plan carefully before setting your primary partners, because changing mid-year costs you 12 months of flexibility. The restriction applies separately to online and in-store primary partner changes.
  • For Vitality Active members, your reward percentage depends on completing health assessments. To earn up to 25% back at your primary partners, each adult on a Vitality Active membership must complete both the Vitality Age online assessment and the Vitality Health Check once every rolling 12 months. If either assessment expires, your reward percentage drops to 10% for your primary partners until you complete both again. The updated percentage applies to qualifying spend in the following month after completion.

What counts as HealthyFood

Not everything in Checkers and Woolworths qualifies. HealthyFood items are specifically designated products - vegetables, fruit, wholegrain staples, lean proteins, fat-free dairy, legumes, and healthy fats. Processed foods, alcohol, tobacco, airtime, and non-food items do not qualify. Discovery's HealthyFood Score measures what proportion of your basket consists of qualifying items - a higher score earns you a better reward rate. Buying more qualifying items in a single basket is more efficient than spreading the same spend across multiple small trips with mixed basket compositions.

Vitality points from HealthyFood spending

In addition to Discovery Miles rewards, buying HealthyFood items earns you up to 12,000 Vitality points per year based on your Vitality HealthyFood Score. These points contribute directly to your annual Vitality status - making consistent HealthyFood shopping at your primary partner one of the most efficient dual-benefit actions in the programme (points for status plus Discovery Miles as cashback).
