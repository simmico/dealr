What is Vitality?

Discovery Vitality is a wellness and rewards programme linked to your Discovery product — medical aid, banking, or life insurance. It rewards healthy behaviour with cashback at partner retailers, gym discounts, and travel perks. The more active you are, the higher your Vitality status and the better your benefits.

Cashback is paid back at the start of the following month.

How to activate HealthyFood

  1. Log in to the Discovery app and navigate to Vitality benefits.
  2. Activate HealthyFood and link your Woolworths WRewards card.
  3. At checkout, swipe both your WRewards card and your Vitality card (or use the app QR code).
  4. Cashback on qualifying HealthyFood items appears on your Discovery statement the following month.

Stacking tip

On a single Woolworths Food shop you can earn Vitality HealthyFood cashback (up to 25%), WRewards points (redeemable as cash on your statement), and — if you bank with FNB — eBucks on your transaction. These three benefits stack independently. Swipe each card at checkout and pay with your FNB card to activate all three simultaneously.

What is The Entertainer?

The Entertainer is a 2-for-1 voucher app with hundreds of participating restaurants, coffee shops, and activity venues across South Africa. You pay for one main meal and the lower-priced main meal at the same table is free. Each voucher can be used once per outlet per year.

How the 2-for-1 works

When you redeem a voucher, the lower-priced main meal in your order is deducted from the bill. This means you and a companion can each order a main course and one is free — always the cheaper one. The saving typically ranges from R80 to R300 depending on the restaurant.

Cost and how to get it free

The Entertainer subscription costs R499 per year if bought independently. FNB Gold account holders and above receive a complimentary annual membership as part of their banking package. Activate it through the FNB app under your account benefits.

How to use the app

  1. Download The Entertainer app and activate your membership (or link via FNB).
  2. Browse participating venues in your area before you book.
  3. Always book through the app — not directly with the restaurant.
  4. Present the voucher on arrival and redeem it when requesting the bill.

How WRewards works

WRewards is Woolworths' loyalty programme. You earn points on every Woolworths purchase — food, clothing, and homeware. Points accumulate and convert to cashback that appears as a credit on your Woolworths account statement monthly.

Linking to Vitality

Link your WRewards card to your Discovery Vitality account through the Discovery app. Once linked, your qualifying HealthyFood purchases at Woolworths Food count towards both your WRewards points and your Vitality HealthyFood cashback.

How personalised pricing works

Xtra Savings uses your purchase history to generate personalised discounts on items you regularly buy. Weekly offers are refreshed in the app and applied automatically when you swipe your card or scan your app at checkout.

How to register

  1. Download the Checkers app and create an Xtra Savings profile.
  2. Alternatively, register in-store at the customer service desk for a physical card.
  3. Activate your weekly offers in the app before you shop — unactivated offers do not apply.

Earning cashback

Clicks ClubCard earns points on every purchase at Clicks. Points convert to cashback — typically at a rate of 2% on standard purchases, with boosted rates on ClubCard-exclusive promotions.

Stacking with Vitality

If you have Discovery Vitality, the HealthyCare benefit at Clicks earns Vitality cashback on top of your ClubCard earnings on qualifying health products.

Earning points

The Dis-Chem Benefit card earns points on all purchases — prescription medication, beauty products, supplements, and baby items.

Stacking with Discovery Vitality HealthyCare

If you have Discovery Vitality, your qualifying health and pharmacy spend at Dis-Chem earns Vitality HealthyCare cashback (up to 25% at Gold status) in addition to your Dis-Chem Benefit points.
