What is Vitality?

Discovery Vitality is a wellness and rewards programme linked to your Discovery product — medical aid, banking, or life insurance. It rewards healthy behaviour with cashback at partner retailers, gym discounts, and travel perks. The more active you are, the higher your Vitality status and the better your benefits.

Cashback is paid back at the start of the following month.

How to activate HealthyFood

  1. Log in to the Discovery app and navigate to Vitality benefits.
  2. Activate HealthyFood and link your Woolworths WRewards card.
  3. At checkout, swipe both your WRewards card and your Vitality card (or use the app QR code).
  4. Cashback on qualifying HealthyFood items appears on your Discovery statement the following month.

Stacking tip

On a single Woolworths Food shop you can earn Vitality HealthyFood cashback (up to 25%), WRewards points (redeemable as cash on your statement), and — if you bank with FNB — eBucks on your transaction. These three benefits stack independently. Swipe each card at checkout and pay with your FNB card to activate all three simultaneously.

What is The Entertainer?

The Entertainer is a 2-for-1 voucher app with hundreds of participating restaurants, coffee shops, and activity venues across South Africa. You pay for one main meal and the lower-priced main meal at the same table is free. Each voucher can be used once per outlet per year.

How the 2-for-1 works

When you redeem a voucher, the lower-priced main meal in your order is deducted from the bill. This means you and a companion can each order a main course and one is free — always the cheaper one. The saving typically ranges from R80 to R300 depending on the restaurant.

Cost and how to get it free

The Entertainer subscription costs R499 per year if bought independently. FNB Gold account holders and above receive a complimentary annual membership as part of their banking package. Activate it through the FNB app under your account benefits — the link to download the Entertainer app is included.

How to use the app

  1. Download The Entertainer app and activate your membership (or link via FNB).
  2. Browse participating venues in your area before you book.
  3. Always book through the app — not directly with the restaurant.
  4. Present the voucher on arrival and redeem it when requesting the bill.

Tip

Use it for regular family meals where you would have paid full price anyway. With hundreds of vouchers available, you can dine at a different restaurant each visit. Plan visits across the year — vouchers expire at year end and do not roll over.

How WRewards works

WRewards is Woolworths' loyalty programme. You earn points on every Woolworths purchase — food, clothing, and homeware. Points accumulate and convert to cashback that appears as a credit on your Woolworths account statement monthly. There is no minimum redemption threshold.

How to redeem

Cashback is automatically credited to your WRewards account at the start of each month and appears on your statement. You can use it against your next Woolworths purchase — swipe your loyalty card at checkout and the balance is applied. You do not need to actively redeem anything.

Linking to Vitality

Link your WRewards card to your Discovery Vitality account through the Discovery app. Once linked, your qualifying HealthyFood purchases at Woolworths Food count towards both your WRewards points and your Vitality HealthyFood cashback. Both benefits apply on the same transaction — you do not have to choose.

Stacking with eBucks

If you bank with FNB and have eBucks activated, you can earn FNB eBucks on your Woolworths spend as well. This means a single Woolworths Food shop can earn WRewards points, Vitality cashback, and eBucks simultaneously — provided you swipe each card and pay with your FNB card.

How personalised pricing works

Xtra Savings uses your purchase history to generate personalised discounts on items you regularly buy. The more consistently you shop at Checkers, the more relevant your offers become. Weekly offers are refreshed in the app and applied automatically when you swipe your card or scan your app at checkout.

How to register

  1. Download the Checkers app and create an Xtra Savings profile.
  2. Alternatively, register in-store at the customer service desk for a physical card.
  3. Activate your weekly offers in the app before you shop — unactivated offers do not apply.

App vs physical card

The app version gives you access to personalised offers and allows you to activate them before shopping. The physical card links to the same account but you will not see or activate offers in advance — they are applied based on your profile. The app is strongly recommended for maximum savings.

Tip

The more consistently you shop Checkers (and the more you stick to a predictable basket), the better your personalised offers become over time. Browse your weekly offers the day before your shop and plan your basket around them.

Earning cashback

Clicks ClubCard earns points on every purchase at Clicks. Points convert to cashback — typically at a rate of 2% on standard purchases, with boosted rates on ClubCard-exclusive promotions. Cashback accumulates and can be redeemed against future purchases at any Clicks store.

How cashback is paid

Your cashback balance is visible on the Clicks app and at checkout. You can choose to redeem it at any time against a qualifying purchase. There is no expiry pressure — your balance carries over indefinitely until you spend it.

ClubCard exclusive pricing

Many Clicks products carry a ClubCard price that is significantly lower than the standard shelf price. This ClubCard price is automatically applied when you swipe your card — no activation required. Check the shelf tag for the ClubCard price before assuming the regular price applies.

Stacking with Dis-Chem

If you shop both Clicks and Dis-Chem, you can earn loyalty cashback at both retailers independently. Some brands run parallel promotions across both chains. If you also have Discovery Vitality, the HealthyCare benefit at Clicks earns Vitality cashback on top of your ClubCard earnings on qualifying health products.

Earning points

The Dis-Chem Benefit card earns points on all purchases — prescription medication, beauty products, supplements, and baby items. Points are earned at a standard rate across the store, with boosted rates on promotional items and Dis-Chem house brands.

Redeeming at checkout

Your points balance is shown at checkout and can be redeemed immediately as a rand-for-rand discount on your bill. There is no minimum redemption amount — even R5 worth of points can be applied. Your cashier will prompt you to redeem if you have a balance.

Stacking with Discovery Vitality HealthyCare

If you have Discovery Vitality, your qualifying health and pharmacy spend at Dis-Chem earns Vitality HealthyCare cashback (up to 25% at Gold status) in addition to your Dis-Chem Benefit points. The two programmes stack on the same transaction — swipe your Vitality card and your Benefit card at checkout.

Tip

Dis-Chem runs regular members-only sales where prices are significantly reduced for Benefit card holders. Download the Dis-Chem app to receive notifications of upcoming sales and plan your larger health and beauty purchases around them.
