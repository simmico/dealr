BANKING PAGES - BEFORE / AFTER CHANGES
Cross-referenced against official bank websites only.
Date: 2026-05-21

====================================================================
FILE: banking-ucount.html
Source: ucount.standardbank.co.za
====================================================================

CHANGE 1 - Grocery partner (WRONG RETAILER)

BEFORE:
"Routing your grocery spend to Pick n Pay and other UCount partner retailers
earns up to 20% back in points on qualifying spend at Tier 4."

AFTER:
"Routing your grocery spend to Checkers and Shoprite (UCount's grocery
partners) earns up to 30% back in points on qualifying instore purchases
at Tier 4."

WHY: The official UCount website lists "Checkers and Shoprite" as grocery
partners with "up to 30% back." Pick n Pay is not listed as a UCount partner.
The cashback percentage was also wrong (20% vs the correct 30%).

--------------------------------------------------------------------

CHANGE 2 - Caltex fuel cashback per litre (WRONG FIGURE)

BEFORE:
"Filling up exclusively at Caltex using your linked Standard Bank card earns
up to R5.00 back in UCount Points per litre at Tier 4."
"On a 50-litre fill-up at Tier 4, that is R250 back in points"

AFTER:
"Filling up exclusively at Caltex using your linked Standard Bank card earns
up to R10 back in UCount Points per litre at Tier 4."
"On a 50-litre fill-up at Tier 4, that is R500 back in points"

WHY: The official UCount website states "Up to R10 back in Rewards Points per
litre of fuel" at Caltex/Astron Energy. The previous R5 figure was outdated.
Note: As of May 2026, a promotional rate of up to R15/litre is in effect until
July 15, 2026.

FILE: banking-greenbacks.html
Source: personal.nedbank.co.za/bank/greenbacks.html
====================================================================

CHANGE 3 - BP fuel cashback amount (MISSING SPECIFIC FIGURE)

BEFORE:
"Swiping your registered Nedbank card at BP accumulates enhanced fuel Greenbacks
into your account, above and beyond the standard earn rate."

AFTER:
"Swiping your registered Nedbank card at BP earns you a flat 25 cents per litre
back in cash - on top of your standard Greenbacks earn rate."
Also added: "Note: the 25c per litre cashback does not apply when paying with
the Greenbacks SHOP Card."

WHY: The official Nedbank Greenbacks page confirms the BP fuel cashback is
specifically "25c cashback for every litre of fuel." The SHOP Card exclusion
is also stated on the official page.

--------------------------------------------------------------------

CHANGE 4 - ATM cash withdrawal method (MISSING REQUIREMENT)

BEFORE:
"...withdraw it at any Nedbank ATM - no voucher codes, no partner portals,
no waiting for a statement credit. Cash. In hand."

AFTER:
"...withdraw it at any Nedbank ATM - no voucher codes, no partner portals,
no waiting for a statement credit. Cash. In hand. To do this, you use your
complimentary Greenbacks SHOP Card - a free American Express chip-and-pin card
issued to Greenbacks members."

WHY: The official site specifies that ATM cash withdrawals use the Greenbacks
SHOP Card specifically. This card is free and included with Greenbacks
membership, but users need to know to use it (not their regular bank card).

--------------------------------------------------------------------

CHANGE 5 - Investment options (MORE SPECIFIC)

BEFORE:
"...transfer your Greenbacks directly into an active Nedbank investment pocket"

AFTER:
"...transfer your Greenbacks directly into a Nedbank investment account,
including JustInvest, Platinum Invest, MoneyTrader, a Unit Trust, or your
MyPocket"

WHY: The official site names the specific investment accounts available
(JustInvest, Platinum Invest, MoneyTrader, Unit Trust, MyPocket).

--------------------------------------------------------------------

CHANGE 6 - Membership fee (MISSING INFORMATION)

BEFORE:
[No mention of Greenbacks membership fee]

AFTER:
Added paragraph: "Greenbacks membership costs R35 per month, though it is
included free on certain qualifying premium Nedbank accounts. Your Greenbacks
never expire, so there is no rush to redeem before a cut-off date."

WHY: The official Nedbank site states "Membership: R35/month (free with certain
premium accounts)." The Dealr page gave the impression Greenbacks was entirely
free to access, which is not accurate for all account holders.
