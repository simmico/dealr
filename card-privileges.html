VISA Card Privileges.

Your Platinum, Signature, or Infinite VISA card carries a separate layer of global benefits that has nothing to do with your bank's rewards programme - and most South Africans have never claimed them.

These come from VISA, not your bank

Every major South African bank issues VISA-branded cards at three premium tiers: Platinum, Signature, and Infinite. The tier printed on your card is not just a status symbol - it determines which VISA network privileges you have access to, independently of whatever rewards programme your bank runs.

Your bank's rewards programme (eBucks, Greenbacks, Live Better, UCount) and your VISA card benefits are completely separate systems. Exhausting your bank's annual lounge allowance, for example, does not affect your VISA lounge access. They are counted separately, run through different platforms, and managed through different portals.

What follows is a full breakdown of what each VISA tier unlocks - and exactly where to activate each benefit.

For Platinum, Signature & Infinite cardholders

VISA SSA Premium Lifestyle Hub

VISA Sub-Saharan Africa operates a dedicated partner incentive platform that unlocks high-tier lifestyle, dining, and retail vouchers for qualifying cardholders.

What you unlock on the platform

Navigate to the VISA SSA Rewards Engine and validate your card to access the full catalogue. Validating triggers card-level eligibility checks and opens your active voucher inventory.

  • Streaming vouchers: Free Spotify Premium trials and multi-month subscription credits for South African premium accounts.
  • Ride and delivery credits: Uber and Uber Eats vouchers tailored to local premium card profiles.
  • Premium dining perks: Exclusive dining benefits at top-tier SA restaurants including Marble, La Colombe, Belly of the Beast, Flames at the Four Seasons, and Oku - covering complimentary MCC on arrival or 15% off your total bill.
  • Luxury retail discounts: 10% to 15% off at FARFETCH, Swarovski, and Montblanc through the platform.

How to access

  1. Navigate to the VISA SSA Rewards Engine.
  2. Enter your premium physical or virtual VISA card number to verify your tier eligibility.
  3. Validate your profile via email OTP and complete a 3DS secure check to unlock the active voucher inventory.

Global airport lounge access

When you have used up your bank's annual lounge allowance - or if your bank does not offer lounge access at all - your VISA card tier gives you a completely separate lounge access entitlement through the Visa Airport Companion app. You can access any lounge available in the app, not restricted to what your bank offers.

Card tier Lounge access Guest policy
VISA Signature Up to 6 complimentary visits per calendar year One free guest per visit
VISA Infinite Unlimited visits for the primary cardholder One companion included per visit

Access is tracked digitally through the Visa Airport Companion app. Register your card, locate participating lounges worldwide, and present the app at the lounge entrance. No physical card required at the lounge desk - the app handles it end to end.

Automatic protection

Comprehensive Travel Insurance

Pay for your international flight with your qualifying VISA card and automatic travel protection activates - completely independent of your medical aid's travel rider.

What is covered

  • Emergency medical expenses: Coverage up to $2,500,000 USD on VISA Infinite profiles for sudden illness, emergency evacuation, and repatriation across trips of up to 90 days.
  • Travel inconveniences: Covers flight cancellations, luggage delays, missed connections, and lost luggage up to a total limit of $7,500 USD.

The activation rule

The entire transport fare or ticket must be paid for completely using your premium VISA card to validate the insurance certificate. Splitting the payment across multiple cards, using points, or using a bank transfer will void the policy. Pay the full ticket amount on your VISA premium card at checkout - that is the trigger.

Coverage is valid for trips of up to 90 days from the departure date. This policy operates separately from your medical aid and does not require any pre-registration or application.

Premium accommodation discounts

VISA provides direct financial benefits on global accommodation for premium cardholders - bypassing retail price points when you book through the correct channels.

  • Booking.com wallet credit: Receive up to 7% back as instant wallet credit on Booking.com when settling accommodation bookings directly with your eligible premium VISA card.
  • VISA Luxury Hotel Collection (VLHC): Infinite and Signature cardholders accessing the VLHC portal receive automatic best-available-rate guarantees, complimentary room upgrades upon arrival, complimentary Wi-Fi, a $25 food and beverage credit, and VIP guest status at participating properties worldwide.

For more accommodation benefits, visit the VISA offers and perks portal.

Purchase protection and extended warranties

VISA Signature and Infinite cardholders receive automatic protection on high-value retail purchases - no registration required at the time of purchase.

  • Purchase protection: Covers repair or replacement costs up to $20,000 USD annually for items purchased with your card that are stolen or accidentally damaged within 90 days of the purchase date.
  • Extended warranty: Automatically doubles the manufacturer's original repair warranty by up to an additional 12 months on electronic goods and luxury consumer appliances. No paperwork required at the time of purchase - the benefit activates simply by paying on the card.
