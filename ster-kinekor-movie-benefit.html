Ster-Kinekor Movie Benefit.

Half-price tickets every time - and your kids under 18 get in free before 7pm.

What you get

After activating the Vitality Ster-Kinekor movie benefit, you get the following discounts on Ster-Kinekor ticket prices every time you go:

  • 50% off - 2D, 3D, and Cinema Nouveau tickets
  • 25% off - Cine Prestige, NT Live, and IMAX tickets

There is no monthly limit on how many times you can use the benefit. You pay your monthly Vitality fee and then the discounted ticket price each visit. Note that some movie offerings may not qualify for a discount based on individual studio requirements - the discount applies to qualifying movies.

You use your Vitality digital card number to book on sterkinekor.com or in the Ster-Kinekor app. Physical movie cards are being phased out in favour of the fully digital card. If your physical card is lost or stolen, your Ster-Kinekor card number is available directly in the Discovery app. Activate the benefit first through the Discovery app before attempting to book.

One firm rule: you can only watch a specific movie once at the Vitality rate. If you see the same film a second time, you pay the full retail price. The discount applies once per title per person, not once per cinema visit.

How to activate

Download or update to the latest version of the Discovery app. Log in and navigate to the Rewards section under Vitality. The main member can activate the benefit for all dependants on their Vitality policy. The spouse on the main member's Vitality Health membership can activate for child dependants under 18 only if they are the child's legal guardian.

Once activated, you receive an SMS notification. You can then access your digital Vitality Ster-Kinekor movie card in the Digital Cards section on the main app menu. iOS users can add the card to their Apple Wallet. Android users view the card in the Discovery app. If you are the main member, your digital card will also display all your dependants' card numbers for family bookings.

Vitality Ster-Kinekor is different to the SK CLUB programme. You can belong to both, but you cannot use the Vitality discount and SK CLUB discount on the same ticket. The discounts cannot be combined.

Active Rewards at Ster-Kinekor

When you achieve your weekly Vitality Active Rewards exercise goal, one of the instant reward options you can choose is a small or medium popcorn from Ster-Kinekor. This is separate from the movie ticket discount and requires no additional activation - it is part of the standard Active Rewards benefit. Simply select it from your available rewards after achieving your weekly exercise goal.

The free kids benefit - and the catch

Child dependants on the main member's Vitality Health membership - specifically those aged 2 to 18 years old - can watch 2D and 3D movies that start before 7pm for free at selected Ster-Kinekor theatres. This applies to standard 2D and 3D formats only - not IMAX, Cine Prestige, or NT Live. For those premium formats, children pay the Vitality-discounted adult rate.

The free benefit does not apply to Vitality Active members. It is exclusively a Vitality Health membership benefit.

Children can attend movies after 7pm but pay the Vitality-discounted adult rate. Children cannot watch a movie if they do not meet the age restriction for that film.

  • It does not renew automatically. The main member or spouse must complete the Kids Vitality Health Review online every 12 months to maintain the free kids benefit. The benefit is valid for 12 months from the date the review is completed. Miss the renewal date - even by a day - and the free access stops immediately, reverting to the standard Vitality-discounted adult ticket rate, with no automatic notification from Discovery. Set a calendar reminder the moment you first activate.
  • Children cannot watch the same movie twice. The same once-per-title rule that applies to adult tickets applies to kids tickets too.

Diarise your Kids Health Review renewal date today. The review is completed on the 'Track and earn points' page when logged in to your Discovery profile. Set a reminder 30 days before expiry - the benefit disappears the day after the 12-month window closes.
