Ster-Kinekor Movie Benefit.

Half-price tickets every time — and your kids under 18 get in free before 7pm.

What you get

As a Vitality Health member, you get a flat 50% off standard ticket prices at Ster-Kinekor — 2D, 3D, and Cinema Nouveau. Every time you go, no monthly limit. Premium formats come in at a still-solid 25% off:

  • 50% off — 2D, 3D, Cinema Nouveau
  • 25% off — IMAX, Cine Prestige, NT Live

You use your Vitality digital card number to book — no physical card needed. Activate it in the Discovery portal first.

The free kids trick — and the catch

Dependent children between the ages of 2 and 18 can watch any movie completely free of charge, as long as the session starts before 7:00 PM. This is one of the most underused benefits in the entire Vitality programme — especially for families who go regularly.

But here's the part most parents miss:

  • It doesn't renew on its own. You have to complete the Kids Vitality Health Review online every 12 months. Miss your anniversary date — even by a day — and your children's free access disappears immediately. They revert to full retail prices at the box office with zero warning. Set a calendar reminder.
  • Vitality Active members don't get this. The free kids movie benefit is exclusively available on Vitality Health plans. If you're on Vitality Active, this section doesn't apply to you — sorry.

Set a reminder now. The annual Kids Health Review is easy to forget. Your kids' free movie access cuts off the moment you miss the deadline — before Discovery even notifies you. Diarise it from the date you first activate.
