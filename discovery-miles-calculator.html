The Basics

Three rules that matter

10:1
The Exchange Rate
10 Discovery Miles always equals R1.00. A R1,000 item is always 10,000 Miles at baseline - before any discounts.
+5%
Per Product, Every Day
Each qualifying Discovery product you hold (Vitality Health, Discovery Bank, Discovery Insure) adds a flat 5% discount. Three products = 15% off any day.
15th
D-Day Doubles It
On the 15th of every month, your total discount automatically doubles for 24 hours. Three products becomes 30% off - the maximum cap.

Earn More

How to get MORE Miles

Credit Card
Get a Discovery Credit Card
A Discovery Bank credit card earns significantly more miles per rand than a standard debit card. Depending on your tier, you can earn more per transaction - making D-Day far more valuable the more products you hold.
Virtual Card
Use Your Virtual Card Online
Your Discovery Bank virtual card (available in the app) earns bonus miles on eligible online purchases. Use it specifically at D-Day partner stores - Takealot, Superbalist, Yuppiechef - to stack your earning rate with your discount tier.
1:100
Know Your Rate and Cap
Physical transactional cards earn 1 mile per R100 spent (Flat Rate). Virtual cards earn more per rand. There is also a monthly miles cap - any spending above your cap earns at the flat rate. Check your Discovery portal for your specific cap before planning a large purchase on the 15th.
