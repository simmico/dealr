The Basics

Three rules that matter

The Exchange Rate
10 Discovery Miles always equals R1.00. A R1,000 item is always 10,000 Miles at baseline — before any discounts.
+5%
Per Product, Every Day
Each qualifying Discovery product you hold (Vitality Health, Discovery Bank, Discovery Insure) adds a flat 5% discount. Three products = 15% off any day.
15th
D-Day Doubles It
On the 15th of every month, your total discount automatically doubles for 24 hours. Three products becomes 30% off — the maximum cap.
