Vitality Gym Benefit.

Big savings on gym membership - but you have to pick one and actually go.

The deal - Virgin Active and Planet Fitness

As a Vitality Health member you can join either Virgin Active or Planet Fitness at substantially discounted monthly rates. There is a hard rule: you pick one. Choosing Virgin Active at a Vitality rate means Planet Fitness reverts to full retail price, and vice versa. There is no way to hold both at discounted rates simultaneously.

The main member or spouse with a Club (single gym) or Local membership can save up to 75% off the standard monthly fee, provided they complete the required number of gym sessions. If you fall below the threshold, the saving drops to a flat 50% off - you still have gym access, but at a reduced discount. Premier and Collection (Virgin Active) or National (Planet Fitness) memberships offer a consistent 50% off regardless of session count.

Child dependants have their own arrangements: at Virgin Active, children from six weeks to 18 years on a Club-V or Club-V Max contract get 50% off the standard fee when the main member or spouse holds a gym benefit. At Planet Fitness, children from 6 months to 18 years get free access at Blue or Signature clubs when the main member has a Local contract there.

Activation cost and access tag

When you first activate the Vitality gym benefit, you pay a once-off activation fee equal to one month's full retail rate for the contract tier you choose. This applies to each adult 18 years or older activating the benefit. You also pay the gym's club access device (swipe card or access tag) fee.

Children joining Club-V or Club-V Max at Virgin Active do not pay an activation fee. Discovery Bank clients aged 18 or older with an active Vitality membership can activate through the Discovery Bank app by tapping the join gym tile - no need to visit the gym first to start the process.

You can only change your gym membership type or home club 60 days after the date of activation. Plan your choice carefully before committing - a change before that window is not permitted.

The 36-sessions-per-year rule

To keep the maximum 75% saving, the main member or spouse on a Club or Local gym membership must complete at least 36 gym sessions in any rolling 12-month period. That is an average of three sessions per month - very achievable, but it requires actually attending.

Discovery looks back over the previous 12 months at the end of every month. If you drop below 36 sessions, your saving reduces from up to 75% to 50% immediately. You return to 75% only once you have accumulated 36 sessions again in the rolling 12-month window - and even then, the improved rate only applies from the following month.

One gym session per day counts toward the requirement - multiple sessions on the same day only count as one. Sessions must be longer than 30 minutes to be valid. Online workouts (via the Virgin Active or Planet Fitness app) also earn Vitality fitness points (50 points per session) but do not count toward the 36-session threshold.

Earn 100 Vitality fitness points for each valid in-gym session per day. Combined with online workouts and step tracking (up to 1,200 Vitality points per month), the gym benefit is also one of the most efficient point-earning mechanisms in the programme. Check the Vitality Gym Tracker on discovery.co.za to monitor your rolling session count - don't wait until month 11 to check.

Track your count monthly. The Discovery portal shows your rolling gym visit history. Each adult gym member can only see their own individual dashboard - the main member cannot see a spouse or dependant's session count. Everyone needs to check their own tracker.

Earn Vitality points for teens at the gym

Children aged 14 to 17 years can earn rewards through the Vitality Active Rewards for Teens programme by completing gym workouts. The main member needs to activate a self-paid benefit to link the child's gym profile to their Vitality profile. You will need the child's ID number, home club, and contract start date. Discovery Vitality can backdate points by up to three months if you link an existing gym membership after the fact.
