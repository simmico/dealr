Fuel Rewards

Earn up to R10* back in Rewards Points per litre of
fuel and oil

Don't let the fuel hike get you down. Get double the fuel Rewards when you fill up at your nearest Astron Energy or participating Caltex service station.

How does the double earn rate work?

To qualify for up to R10* back in Rewards Points per litre of fuel and oil, you need to use your qualifying Standard Bank Credit card to pay for:

  • 100% of qualifying POS spend to be done on a qualifying Standard Bank Credit Card
  • 100% fuel spend at participating Astron Energy and/or Caltex service stations per Fixed Cycle (Zero fuel spend at any other fuel service stations)
  • Have a transactional current account and 2 other Standard Bank products in different categories. These categories are:
    • Home Loan
    • Personal Loan
    • Insurance
    • Wills
    • Offshore Banking
    • Online Share Trading
    • Standard Bank Mobile
    • Vehicle and Asset Finance (VAF)
    • Credit Card
    • Savings & Investments
    • Services

UCount Rewards:
Stay vigilant!

Fraudsters are masquerading as UCount Rewards through phishing emails and smishing SMSs linked to the redemption of Rewards Points. Don't click on any link, open an attachment, provide an OTP or scan any QR codes to redeem your Rewards Points on such email or SMS. Report suspected fraud to 0800 222 050.
