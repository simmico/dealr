About Dealr.

Built in South Africa, for South Africans.

What Dealr is

Dealr is a curated platform for South African deals, promotions, and loyalty benefits. Not a price comparison site. Not an affiliate farm. A clean, honest resource for people who want to get more value from their everyday spending.

We find deals that are actually worth knowing about, verify them before they go live, and add a tip on how to get the most from each one. We cover groceries, restaurants, birthday perks, subscriptions, and banking benefits.

Why we built it

South African deals platforms have not kept up. They are cluttered, outdated, and increasingly irrelevant to how South Africans actually shop and spend. Dealr is the alternative we wanted to exist.

Most South Africans are sitting on benefits they have never activated — from Vitality cashback to Entertainer 2-for-1 deals to birthday perks at their favourite restaurants. Dealr makes that knowledge accessible in one place.
