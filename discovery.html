Discovery Miles D-Day

Every 15th of the month, Discovery activates Miles D-Day - your product discounts automatically double for 24 hours. With all three qualifying products (Vitality Health, Discovery Bank, Discovery Insure), that's 30% off at Takealot, Superbalist, Yuppiechef, and more.

Hold your miles for the 15th. Spending on any other day leaves real savings on the table. Use the calculator to see exactly how much you save.
