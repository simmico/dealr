The Bigger Picture

The rewards gap most South Africans are missing

Every major fuel station in South Africa has at least one loyalty mechanism. Most South Africans use none of them — or only one when they could be stacking two or three simultaneously. A single 50-litre fill at BP with the right combination of cards can return R15 to R40 in cashback, Smart Shopper points, and Discovery Miles simultaneously. Over a year, that compounds significantly.

The most important insight: most loyalty programmes at the pump are designed to be stacked. You swipe your station loyalty card and your bank or partner card at the same time. These are different systems counting independently. None of the programmes below exclude the others.

The guides in this hub cover every major programme worth knowing. The calculator shows you exactly what each fill earns based on your cards and tier. Use it before your next fill-up.
