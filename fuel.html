SA Fuel Rewards Hub.

Fuel prices are high. Here is how to make every litre work harder — with a live calculator showing exactly what you earn at every pump.

The rewards gap most South Africans are missing

Every major fuel station in South Africa has at least one loyalty mechanism. Most South Africans use none of them - or only one when they could be stacking two or three simultaneously. A single 50-litre fill at BP with the right combination of cards can return R15 to R40 in cashback, Smart Shopper points, and Discovery Miles simultaneously. Over a year, that compounds.

The most important insight: most loyalty programmes at the pump are designed to be stacked. You swipe your station loyalty card and your bank/partner card at the same time. These are different systems counting independently. None of the programmes below exclude the others.

The page below covers every major programme worth knowing, ordered from best to worst. The calculator at the bottom shows you exactly what each fill earns based on your cards and tier.

Best overall loyalty

BP Rewards

The most stackable fuel programme in SA. One base card plus two possible partner add-ons, each running independently on the same transaction.

Base earn rate

Swipe your BP Rewards card at any participating BP station to earn 20c per litre in cashback on fuel. You also earn 1% cashback on all purchases at bp Express convenience stores. The card is free to register at bprewards.co.za or via the QR code on any BP Rewards physical card.

The double-swipe strategy

At the pump you can swipe your BP Rewards card and one partner card at the same time. Each counts independently. Choose the combination that matches the partner cards you carry:

Your combination Cashback / reward per litre Programmes involved
BP Rewards only 20c/l BP Rewards
BP Rewards + PnP Smart Shopper 30c/l combined + 10 Smart Shopper points/l BP Rewards + PnP Smart Shopper
BP Rewards + Nedbank Greenbacks 45c/l combined (20c + 25c) BP Rewards + Nedbank
BP Rewards + Discovery Insure Vitality Drive 20c/l BP base + up to 50% of monthly fuel spend back via Vitality Drive BP Rewards + Discovery Insure

Note: Smart Shopper and Discovery Insure cards cannot be swiped together on the same transaction - you choose one partner per fill-up. Nedbank Greenbacks requires an eligible Nedbank card or the BP-linked Nedbank product.

Discovery Insure Vitality Drive at BP

If you have Discovery Insure with Vitality Drive, BP is one of the best places to fill up in SA. Your driving behaviour each month earns Vitality Drive points, and those points determine your cashback percentage - up to 50% of your monthly BP fuel spend back. The better you drive, the more you earn.

  • Discovery Bank Vitality Money clients can also earn up to 20% back in Discovery Miles on fuel spend at BP, on top of the Vitality Drive cashback.
  • Discovery Miles earn stacks with the BP Rewards 20c/l base simultaneously.
  • Install the Vitality Drive sensor at any Tiger Wheel & Tyre or Discovery Drive Centre to activate.

PnP Smart Shopper at BP

Earn 10 Smart Shopper points per litre of fuel when you swipe your PnP Smart Shopper card at BP - in addition to the BP Rewards 20c/l base. Smart Shopper points accumulate and convert to real rand cashback at Pick n Pay tills. You can also redeem R20 or more in Smart Shopper points toward your fuel payment at participating BP stations linked to the PnP Smart Shopper programme.

Redemption and programme rules

  • Minimum R20 cashback balance to redeem at the pump or BP Express.
  • Points do not expire while your account is active (at least one earn or redeem transaction in any 3-month window).
  • Inactive accounts: points expire after 12 months of no activity.
  • Virtual card available in digital wallet — no physical card required to earn.

Shell

Shell V+ Rewards

Free to join, double-swipe enabled, and compatible with Discovery Vitality Drive for the same up-to-50% monthly cashback as BP.

How V+ Rewards works

Shell V+ Rewards is free to register - no subscription fee, no monthly cost. Registration takes under 3 minutes at any participating Shell station or via the Shell website. Once registered, swipe your V+ card at the pump every time you fill up.

Double-swipe with Capitec

Shell's primary partner for the double-swipe is Capitec. Swipe your V+ card and pay with your Capitec card simultaneously to earn:

  • 20c per litre cashback on fuel (V+ base rate)
  • 0.5% cashback on selected in-store purchases at Shell Select

Cashback is stored in your V+ balance. Redeem by swiping your V+ card and entering your PIN at the Shell pump or shop - no minimum balance threshold.

Discovery Vitality Drive at Shell

Shell is a Vitality Drive partner - you earn the same up to 50% of your monthly Shell fuel spend back based on your driving behaviour score. The mechanism is identical to BP: better driving behaviour earns more Vitality Drive points, which translates to a higher cashback percentage on your monthly fuel spend.

  • Requires Discovery Insure with Vitality Drive sensor installed.
  • Link your Vitality Drive card when filling up at Shell to track the earn.
  • Vitality Drive cashback runs independently from V+ Rewards — both count on the same fill.
Engen

Engen: eBucks & Clicks Clubcard

For FNB/RMB clients at higher reward levels, Engen offers the highest straight per-litre eBucks rate in SA - up to R8 per litre at Level 5.

FNB eBucks at Engen

FNB and RMB Private Bank clients earn eBucks every time they fill up at participating Engen stations. The earn rate scales significantly by reward level - this is where being at a higher FNB reward level pays off most visibly at the pump:

FNB Reward Level eBucks per litre 50 litres earns
Level 1R0.50R25.00 in eBucks
Level 2R1.00R50.00 in eBucks
Level 3R2.00R100.00 in eBucks
Level 4R4.00R200.00 in eBucks
Level 5R8.00R400.00 in eBucks

eBucks are worth face value at participating eBucks partners. Pay at Engen using your eBucks card at the pump or via Pay in eBucks in the FNB App.

Clicks Clubcard at Engen

Swipe your Clicks Clubcard when paying for fuel at participating Engen stations to earn 1 Clicks Clubcard point per litre. Points accumulate across all Clicks purchases and convert to cashback on the 20th of each month. The per-litre rate is modest but requires zero additional action if you already have a Clicks Clubcard.

The FNB Level 5 case

For FNB clients at Level 5, the Engen eBucks rate (R8/l) is the single highest per-litre fuel reward rate available from any programme in SA on a standalone basis. A 50-litre fill earns R400 in eBucks. If you are already at Level 5, making Engen your default fill-up station is the single highest-value fuel decision you can make.

Astron Energy / Caltex

UCount Rewards at Astron Energy

Standard Bank UCount's dedicated fuel partner. Earn rate scales dramatically by tier - Tier 5 credit card clients earn up to R5/l base (R10/l with double-reward conditions).

UCount fuel earn rates

UCount Rewards has a dedicated fuel category for Standard Bank credit and debit card spend at participating Astron Energy and Caltex stations. Rates differ significantly between credit and debit - use your Standard Bank credit card for every fill to earn the higher rate:

UCount Tier Credit card (per litre) 50 litres earns
Tier 1R0.30R15.00
Tier 2R1.00R50.00
Tier 3R2.50R125.00
Tier 4R3.50R175.00
Tier 5R5.00R250.00

Double reward: Eligible clients who meet all five Standard Bank criteria simultaneously (all spend on SB credit card, all fuel at Astron Energy, active transactional account, two or more other SB products) can earn double the above rates - up to R10 per litre at Tier 5.

Astron Energy Daily Rewards

Astron Energy also runs its own Daily Rewards programme (powered by Payment24) separate from UCount. Every fill-up enters you into an instant-win draw for fuel vouchers or convenience store vouchers - redeemable on the spot. Register at the forecourt or via admin.payment24.co/AstronRewards. It is a chance-based system rather than a fixed earn rate, so treat it as a bonus on top of UCount rather than a primary strategy.

What you can do with UCount Rewards Points

  • Pay for fuel directly at Astron Energy / Caltex pumps using your UCount balance
  • Spend at Checkers, Shoprite, and Checkers Sixty60
  • Pay for parking and airport lounge access
  • Invest into a Tax-Free Savings Account (TFSA) - the standout UCount feature
  • Contribute to a personal loan repayment
  • Donate to education causes

Sasol and Total Energy

Sasol and Total Energy operate fuel stations across South Africa but run limited standalone loyalty programmes compared to the options above. Sasol participates in some banking partner cashback campaigns through select card products, but does not operate a dedicated loyalty card with a competitive per-litre earn rate. Total Energy similarly offers minimal structured rewards.

If you have no loyalty commitments to a particular station, choosing BP, Shell, Engen, or Astron Energy over Sasol and Total exclusively for the loyalty benefits will result in meaningfully more reward value over time. Fill at Sasol or Total when convenient, but do not build a loyalty strategy around either.
