CHANGE 1 - Vitality Active plan cap (INCORRECT DATA)
Source: discovery.co.za/vitality/healthyfood

BEFORE:
"Standard Vitality Health members can earn up to 25% back. If you're on the
cheaper Vitality Active plan (R159/mo per adult), you're permanently capped
at 10% - no matter how much healthy food you buy."

AFTER:
"All Vitality members can earn up to 25% back at their designated Primary
Partner. If you're on Vitality Active, you can only designate one Primary
Partner store - you won't earn anything at the other. Vitality Health members
can designate a Primary Partner for both Checkers and Woolworths, earning 25%
at one and 10% at the other."

WHY: Discovery's official site confirms Vitality Active members CAN earn up to
25% at their single designated Primary Partner. The old text incorrectly said
they were capped at 10%.

--------------------------------------------------------------------

CHANGE 2 - Woolworths loyalty card name (OUTDATED NAME)
Source: discovery.co.za/vitality/healthyfood

BEFORE:
"For Woolworths, link your WRewards Card via the Discovery App."

AFTER:
"For Woolworths, link your MyDifference card via the Discovery App."

WHY: Woolworths rebranded their loyalty card from WRewards to MyDifference.
The Discovery website now references "Woolworths MyDifference cards."

Source: discovery.co.za/vitality/healthy-food-studio/

"The HealthyFood Studio runs in-person and live online cooking classes led by

CHANGE 5 - "Pop-up locations" claim and pricing (INACCURATE/MISSING)
Source: discovery.co.za/vitality/healthy-food-studio/

BEFORE:
"Classes are run throughout the year at the studio and occasionally at pop-up
locations. Prices vary per class - check the booking site for what's on."
