Capitec Live Better.

No tiers, no complexity, no monthly reward fee. Real rands deposited straight into your savings pocket - and a Shell fuel perk most Capitec clients never activate.

How Live Better works

Capitec Live Better is a free rewards programme available to all Capitec customers. You activate it through the Capitec banking app at no cost - there is no monthly fee, no tier to unlock, and no points currency to manage. What you earn comes back as real cash deposited directly into your Live Better savings account on the 10th of each month - Live Better Day.

The programme has four pillars: Bank Better (cash back on credit card spend), Spend Better (partner rewards and discounts), Save Better (automated savings tools), and MoneyUp (financial education). Each pillar adds value independently, so you benefit even if you only use one or two of them.

There is no loyalty currency depreciation and no conversion rate. One rand earned is one rand deposited. This is the clearest advantage Capitec has over points-based programmes - there is nothing to decode.

Bank Better - cash back on your credit card

Capitec credit card holders earn 1% cash back on their monthly credit card spend. To qualify, your credit card must be in good standing. Cash back is deposited into your Live Better savings account on the 10th of each month.

Capitec Connect SIM holders also receive 1GB of free data every month, also credited on the 10th. If you use a Capitec Connect SIM and have a Capitec credit card, both benefits run simultaneously with no additional effort required.

To maximise the cash back, route as much of your monthly spending through your Capitec credit card as possible. Every rand spent on the card earns; spending on your debit card does not earn the 1% credit card cash back.

Spend Better - partner rewards

The Spend Better section of Live Better gives you cash back and exclusive discounts at a growing list of partner retailers. Partners confirmed in the programme include Dis-Chem, Cashbuild, Tiger Wheel and Tyre, Exclusive Books, Dragonpass, Spotify, and Educate24.

Partner rewards are deposited on the 10th of the month alongside your credit card cash back. Some partner offers apply to all Capitec cardholders, while others specifically require a Capitec credit card - check the individual offer in the app for the requirement.

The app is the primary source of truth for current partner campaigns. Capitec updates partner promotions regularly, so it is worth checking the Live Better section in the app each month to see what is active. Dis-Chem in particular runs recurring cashback campaigns that change periodically.

Save Better - Round-Up and Interest Sweep

Activating Round-Up in the Capitec app is one of the most effective passive savings habits in South African retail banking. Once enabled, the app automatically rounds every transaction up to the nearest R10, R20, or R50 - depositing the difference straight into your Live Better savings pocket. Set it once and the money moves automatically.

Interest Sweep is the companion tool. It moves surplus funds from your transactional account into your Live Better savings pocket at the end of each day, earning interest on money that would otherwise sit idle. Together, Round-Up and Interest Sweep can grow your Live Better savings balance meaningfully without any active effort on your part.

Both tools are free to activate and can be switched on or off at any time through the Capitec banking app.

MoneyUp - financial education with value

MoneyUp is Capitec's financial education component within Live Better. It provides structured content on budgeting, credit, savings, and financial planning. While it is not a cashback mechanism on its own, completing MoneyUp courses can unlock additional rewards or campaigns - check the app for current incentives tied to completion milestones.

Why simplicity is the point

Capitec Live Better is not the highest-earning programme in absolute terms. FNB eBucks at Level 5 can generate more rand value for someone who optimises aggressively across multiple product holdings. But the absence of fees, tiers, and management overhead makes Live Better the most accessible programme for the majority of South Africans.

The two actions that cost nothing and start working immediately: activate Round-Up in the app, and check Spend Better for active partner offers. Both take under five minutes and deliver returns from day one.
