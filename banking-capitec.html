Capitec Live Better.

No tiers, no complexity, no monthly reward fee. Real rands deposited straight into your savings pocket — and a Shell fuel perk most Capitec clients never activate.

How Live Better works

Capitec takes a fundamentally different approach to banking rewards. Rather than points, tiers, or wallet credits, Live Better pays direct cash into an automated savings pocket every month. There is no conversion rate to understand, no expiry to track, and no third-party portal to navigate. What you earn is what you receive — in rands.

The value anchor is a 1:1 rand representation. Every cent of cashback is exactly that: a cent. There is no loyalty currency depreciation and no conversion tax.

There is also no monthly reward membership fee. Unlike FNB eBucks or Standard Bank UCount, which require a paid tier to unlock meaningful benefits, Capitec's programme is entirely free to access from any qualifying account.

The round-up automation

Activating the round-up feature in the Capitec app is one of the most effective passive savings habits available in South African retail banking. Once enabled, the app automatically rounds every transaction up to the nearest R10, R20, or R50 — depositing the difference straight into your Live Better savings pocket.

The app then applies automated monthly bonus interest to that savings pocket, compounding the value of what you set aside without any additional effort. Over a month of active spending, the round-up accumulation adds up to a meaningful saving without any behavioural change on your part.

Set it once and forget it. The money moves automatically and the interest compounds in the background.

Shell forecourt cashback

Capitec has a direct fuel partnership with Shell service stations. When you swipe your registered Capitec card at any Shell forecourt alongside a Shell V-Plus loyalty card, you receive a flat 20 cents per litre back in cash.

This sounds modest per litre, but on a 50-litre tank that is R10 cash back per fill. For a driver filling weekly, that is over R500 per year from fuel alone — on top of the standard Live Better cashback on the transaction itself.

The key step most people miss: you need both your Capitec card and your Shell V-Plus card active at the point of sale. The Shell V-Plus card is free and available at any Shell forecourt. If you do not have one, that is the starting point.

Why simplicity is the point

Capitec Live Better is not the highest-earning programme in absolute terms. FNB eBucks at Level 5 can generate more value for someone who optimises aggressively. But the absence of fees, tiers, and management overhead makes Live Better the most accessible programme for the majority of South Africans.

If you bank with Capitec and have not activated the round-up feature or the Shell partnership, those are two genuinely easy gains with no downside and no cost. Start there.
