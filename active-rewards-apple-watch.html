Apple Watch & Wearables.

Walk out of the iStore with a new device at R0 upfront - powered entirely by your exercise goals.

What the benefit offers

The Vitality Active Rewards with Apple Watch benefit lets you walk out of an iStore with either an Apple Watch Series SE 3 GPS Aluminium Case Sport 40mm or an Apple Watch Series 11 GPS Aluminium Case Sport 42mm - funded over 24 months based entirely on whether you achieve your weekly Vitality Active Rewards exercise goals.

If you achieve all your weekly goals every month for 24 months, Discovery Vitality funds the watch completely. If you miss goals, a monthly penalty is deducted from your linked qualifying Discovery Bank Suite. The penalty scales with how many goals you miss each month.

How the funding works - goal by goal

Each month contains up to 4 weekly personalised exercise goals. The table below shows how Discovery calculates your monthly penalty based on the number of goals you achieve:

Weekly goals achieved that month Penalty deducted from your account Discovery covers
4 goals (all)0%100%
3 goals50%50%
2 goals75%25%
0-1 goals100%0%

For example: based on an Apple Watch retail price of R9,899 at time of collection (illustrative), the maximum monthly penalty is approximately R412.46 (100% penalty month). If you achieve 3 of 4 goals, approximately R206.23 is deducted (50% penalty). Hit all 4 goals and nothing is deducted that month.

Penalty deductions occur automatically on the 10th of each following month. Discovery Vitality starts calculating goals from the first full month after you collect the watch. If you collect in October, November is your first calculation month, and the first potential deduction goes off on 10 December.

Who qualifies and what you need before activating

  • You must be a primary accountholder on a qualifying Discovery Bank Suite: Gold, Platinum, Black, Purple, or Purple Wealth.
  • Vitality Active Rewards must be activated on your profile before you can apply for the Apple Watch benefit.
  • Your Discovery Bank product must be in good standing.
  • A minimum spend of R3,000 on your qualifying Discovery Bank credit card is required in the month before activation.
  • New Discovery Bank clients or those who have recently upgraded must have transacted with their credit card for at least three months before activating.
  • Only one Apple Watch benefit per Vitality Health policy can be active in a 24-month period - either the main member, spouse, or one adult dependant aged 21 or older may hold it, not multiple people simultaneously.
  • Your Discovery Bank Suite limit must be at least R15,000 when you collect the watch, with an available balance of at least R5,000.
  • Apple Watch requires iPhone 11 or later (including iPhone SE 2nd generation or later) running the latest iOS version.

How to activate - step by step

  1. Download or update to the latest version of the Discovery app and log in on your iPhone, or go to www.discovery.co.za.
  2. Navigate to the Apple Watch page under Vitality Active Rewards and click Get started.
  3. Confirm your details and pay the non-refundable R1,199 activation fee using your qualifying Discovery Bank Suite. This is deducted within 24 hours.
  4. You receive a digital proof of payment with a unique redemption code. Use this to collect your Apple Watch at your nearest iStore. The proof of payment is valid for 90 days from date of issue - if you do not collect within 90 days, it expires and you would need to pay the activation fee again.
  5. Once you have the watch, link it to Vitality through the Discovery app (Vitality section - Devices and apps) to start earning fitness points toward your weekly exercise goals.

The R1,199 activation fee is non-refundable under all circumstances - including if you do not redeem the proof of payment or if it expires.

What to watch out for

  • Weekly goals are dynamic and personalised. They adjust upwards and downwards based on your past activity levels and goal achievement. Goals are designed to be achievable, but if they feel too high or too low for your current fitness level, speak to Vitality. Do not wait until you are already missing multiple months.
  • Illness and travel do not pause the benefit. If you cannot achieve your goals for any reason - including genuine illness or travel - you are still liable for the monthly penalty. The commitment is unconditional.
  • Extended warranties are not included. The Apple Watch warranty from Apple is 12 months, and the benefit runs for 24 months. Discovery recommends taking out the iCare extended warranty from iStore to cover the second year - at your own cost.
  • You cannot transfer the benefit to a dependant mid-term, or vice versa. Only one benefit runs per policy per 24-month period.
  • Deductions happen on the 10th of the month. If the 10th falls on a weekend or public holiday, Discovery deducts a day earlier or later. Make sure your account has funds available.
  • Wait at least 30 minutes after a workout before syncing to the Discovery app. Syncing too soon after a session can cause inaccurate point allocation.

Track your goals every week, not every month. The goal cycle runs Friday midnight to the following Friday midnight. Check your progress mid-week so you have time to get an extra session in if you are falling short - not on Thursday evening when it may be too late.
