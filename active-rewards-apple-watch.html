Apple Watch & Wearables.

Walk out of the iStore with a new device at R0 upfront - powered entirely by your exercise goals.

How the funding works

Discovery lets you pick up an Apple Watch or Garmin at any iStore without paying a cent upfront. They fund the device over 24 months - but how much they cover each month depends entirely on how many of your weekly exercise goals you hit.

Each month contains up to 4 weekly personalised exercise goals. Hit all four, Discovery pays 100% of that month's installment. Miss all four, and 100% comes off your linked card. It's that direct.

Goals hit that month Discovery pays You owe
4 goals100%0%
3 goals75%25%
2 goals50%50%
1 goal25%75%
0 goals0%100%

What to watch out for

The benefit is genuinely great - but there are a few things that catch people off guard.

  • Even 1 goal out of 4 cuts your liability to 75%. Never let a busy month roll by without hitting at least one goal. A short walk counts.
  • Extended warranties (like iCare) aren't covered. If you want that protection, you pay for it in full out of your own pocket. Worth thinking about on a device you're committed to for 24 months.
  • The deduction is automatic. If you have a bad month, there's no grace period and no call from Discovery first. The money comes off your linked account. Keep an eye on your goals dashboard.

Quick tip: Your personalised goals are set based on your fitness level - they're meant to be achievable. If yours feel too hard or too easy, you can speak to Vitality about adjusting them. Don't wait until you're already failing.
