The golden rule

Register before you need it

Most SA birthday programmes require you to register your birthday at least 30 days in advance. Do not wait until your birthday month.

Many require the brand's app to claim. Download them now and register while you remember.

Some are valid on your birthday only. Others give you a birthday week or even the entire birthday month. Check the specific brand.

Pro tip: Set a calendar reminder every year in the month before your birthday to activate all your perks. Some programmes reset annually and require re-activation.
