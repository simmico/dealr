Shell V+ Rewards.

Free to join and compatible with Discovery Vitality Drive — the two programmes that make Shell worth prioritising if you drive with Discovery Insure.

Shell V+ Rewards

How V+ Works

A straightforward cashback card — free to register, no monthly subscription, earns 20c per litre on fuel.

Shell V+ Rewards is free to register — no subscription fee, no monthly cost. Registration takes under 3 minutes at any participating Shell station or via the Shell website. Once registered, swipe your V+ card at the pump every time you fill up to earn.

Base earn rate

Earn 20c per litre of fuel cashback on every fill-up at participating Shell stations. Cashback is stored in your V+ balance. Redeem by presenting your V+ card and entering your PIN at the Shell pump or Shell Select shop — no minimum balance required to redeem.

Discovery Insure

Vitality Drive at Shell

Shell is a Vitality Drive partner — the same up-to-50% monthly cashback mechanic that applies at BP also applies at Shell.

If you have Discovery Insure with Vitality Drive, Shell is one of the best filling stations available. Your monthly driving behaviour earns Vitality Drive points, which determine your cashback percentage on your total monthly Shell fuel spend. The better you drive, the more you earn back at the end of each month.

The mechanism is identical to BP: better driving behaviour earns more Vitality Drive points, which translates to a higher cashback percentage on your monthly fuel spend. Both programmes can be active simultaneously if you split fills between BP and Shell — each station's spend is tracked and cashback calculated independently.

What to Know

Shell's Loyalty Partners

Shell V+ partners with Discovery Vitality Drive for the high-value monthly cashback mechanism. This is the combination that delivers the most meaningful returns at Shell for qualifying clients.

Capitec does not currently offer a fuel benefit at Shell or at any fuel station. Capitec's Live Better rewards programme does not include a fuel cashback component. If you are a Capitec client looking for fuel rewards, the best available options are through FNB eBucks at Engen, UCount at Astron Energy, or BP Rewards with your current banking card.

The V+ card is free and takes minutes to register. Even if Discovery Vitality Drive is not currently active for you, earning 20c/l in V+ base cashback costs nothing and accumulates over time.
