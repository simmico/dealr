Redemption

How to Earn and Redeem at the Pump

Earning eBucks at Engen

To earn eBucks on fuel, swipe your FNB eBucks card at the Engen pump when filling up. The eBucks are credited to your FNB eBucks balance — visible in the FNB App or on the eBucks website.

Redeeming eBucks at Engen

There are two ways to use your eBucks at Engen:

  • Swipe eBucks card at the pump: Inform the attendant you want to Pay in eBucks, then swipe your eBucks card at the point of sale terminal. Your eBucks balance covers the fuel purchase up to your available balance.
  • Pay in eBucks via FNB App: Open the FNB App, navigate to Pay in eBucks, and select Engen as the partner. Generate a payment code and present it at the Engen cashier or pay terminal. This method works for fuel and in-store purchases at participating Engen QuickShops.

Tip: If you are not yet at FNB Reward Level 5, check the FNB App for which qualifying actions would move you up. Higher reward levels are often achievable by consolidating your banking relationship with FNB.

Bonus Programme

Clicks Clubcard at Engen

Swipe your Clicks Clubcard when paying for fuel at participating Engen stations to earn 1 Clicks Clubcard point per litre. Points accumulate across all Clicks purchases and convert to cashback on the 20th of each month.

The per-litre value is modest — approximately 1 cent per litre — but requires zero additional effort if you already carry a Clicks Clubcard. If you regularly earn eBucks at Engen, adding a Clicks Clubcard swipe on the same transaction costs nothing and adds a small ongoing return.

  • 50 litres earns 50 Clicks points — approximately R0.50 in cashback value.
  • Points accumulate and convert automatically to cashback on the 20th — redeemable in-store at Clicks.
  • No minimum transaction requirement to earn Clicks points at Engen.
The headline number

The FNB Level 5 Case

For FNB clients at Reward Level 5, the Engen eBucks rate of R8 per litre is the single highest per-litre fuel reward rate available from any programme in South Africa on a standalone basis. A 50-litre fill earns R400 in eBucks. A 100-litre fill earns R800 in eBucks.

If you are already at FNB Reward Level 5, making Engen your default fill-up station is the single highest-value fuel decision available. The eBucks earned in one year of regular fills compound into a meaningful sum redeemable at a wide range of eBucks partners.
