HealthyFood Cooking Studio.

Professional cooking masterclasses from Discovery's own chefs - and no, you don't need a Vitality membership to join.

What the HealthyFood Studio is

The HealthyFood Studio is Discovery Vitality's dedicated culinary education space. It hosts in-person and online cooking classes led by qualified chefs, focusing on transforming whole, unprocessed ingredients into genuinely delicious and nutritious meals. The philosophy is that healthy eating does not require compromising on flavour - the studio's approach is culinary first, with nutrition integrated throughout.

Classes cover a wide range of themes: global cuisines, healthy baking, pasta, couples' classes, kids' classes, and specialty courses for specific dietary needs such as cooking for vegetarians, diabetics, or those managing heart conditions. The studio also occasionally hosts masterclasses with celebrity chefs and private events including team building, bridal parties, and birthday celebrations.

No Vitality membership required. You do not need to be a Discovery Vitality member to attend a HealthyFood Studio class. The classes are open to the public. Anyone aged 8 or older can attend.

What to expect in an in-person class

In-studio classes follow a structured format:

  • Registration and welcome drinks on arrival
  • The chef explains cooking theory relevant to the session's theme
  • A live cooking demonstration by the chef
  • Hands-on cooking where attendees apply what they have learnt
  • Meals are served and enjoyed by all attendees together

Classes take place in the morning and evening throughout the year. Costs vary by class type. View the current schedule, pricing, and available time slots on the HealthyFood Studio website and secure your booking directly there.

Arrive at least 15 minutes before your class starts - sessions begin promptly at the advertised time and late arrivals may miss the introductory content. If you are absent for any part of the course, there are no catch-up arrangements or partial refunds for missed portions.

Online classes

The HealthyFood Studio also hosts online cooking classes, which follow the same structure as in-studio classes but are conducted virtually. The same refund policy applies to online classes as to in-person sessions. All cancellations must be communicated at least 48 hours before the class starts to be eligible for a reschedule or refund.

Transferring your booking

Bookings are transferable. If you have made a booking in your name but cannot attend, someone else can attend in your place. You do not need to be the person who made the booking in order to attend - just notify the studio if the name of the attendee will differ from the booking name.

Before you book - read this

  • Cancellations within 48 hours are non-refundable. The refund policy requires that cancellations be communicated at least 48 hours before the class starts. Classes booked within 48 hours of commencement are non-refundable from the moment of booking, as the 48-hour window will already have passed. If you cannot cancel in time, you can send someone in your place instead.
  • The studio can cancel classes with low enrolment. The HealthyFood Studio reserves the right to cancel a class if there are insufficient attendees. If this happens, they will make every effort to place you on an alternative class at a time convenient for you.
  • Photography and video consent is automatic on booking. By booking and participating in a class, you grant the HealthyFood Studio and Discovery the right to photograph and video record you - and any children you bring - for social media and marketing purposes. If you do not want to appear in their content, tell the staff in person before the class begins. This must be done in person and before the session starts - not via email afterwards.

Contact and booking

For questions about the HealthyFood Studio, call 011 529 6527 or visit the website directly to see the current schedule and make a booking. Follow the studio on Instagram at vitality_healthyfoodstudio for class previews and new session announcements.
