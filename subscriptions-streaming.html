Streaming.

Get Netflix, Showmax, and Prime Video free or heavily discounted — through rewards you may already have.

FNB eBucks — Netflix & Showmax

FNB Gold and above account holders can redeem eBucks towards Netflix Standard or Showmax subscriptions each month. At eBucks Level 4 and 5, the full subscription can be covered — effectively making it free. Log in to the eBucks portal and navigate to Entertainment > Streaming to redeem.

Tip: Ensure your eBucks level is high enough before month-end. Level 3 gives you a partial discount; Level 4+ covers the full subscription.

Nedbank Greenbacks — Showmax

Nedbank credit and cheque card holders on qualifying accounts can redeem Greenbacks points for a Showmax subscription. The redemption rate is approximately 5,000 Greenbacks for one month of Showmax Standard. Visit the Nedbank Money app and navigate to Greenbacks > Streaming.

Tip: Greenbacks accumulate faster if you use your Nedbank card for everyday spend. Grocery and fuel purchases earn the most points.

MTN & Vodacom Contracts — Netflix Included

Several MTN and Vodacom postpaid contract tiers include a Netflix or Showmax subscription as a value-add at no extra charge. When signing or renewing a contract, look for deals labelled "Netflix included" or "Showmax bundle". The streaming service is billed to your contract and usually runs for the duration of the contract period.

Tip: Negotiate at upgrade time. If the standard offer does not include streaming, ask specifically — dealers often have flexibility to add it.

Discovery Vitality — Showmax Benefit

Discovery Health members on certain plan options receive a complimentary Showmax subscription as part of their Vitality benefit. The benefit activates through the Discovery app under Vitality > Entertainment. You link your Showmax account and the monthly fee is covered automatically while the benefit is active.

Tip: Check your specific plan type in the Discovery app. The Showmax benefit is available on Executive and Core plan options and above on most medical aid plan tiers.
