Astron Energy / Caltex.

Standard Bank UCount's dedicated fuel partner — with credit and debit card options, tiered earn rates, and a double-rewards mechanism for qualifying clients.

Standard Bank UCount

UCount Standard Fuel Rewards

Earn rates scale significantly by UCount tier. Credit card rates are substantially higher than debit card rates.

UCount Rewards has a dedicated fuel category for Standard Bank credit and debit card spend at participating Astron Energy and Caltex stations. The earn rate scales dramatically from Tier 1 to Tier 5, making higher UCount tiers very valuable at the pump.

UCount Tier Credit card (per litre) Debit card (per litre) 50L credit earns
Tier 1R0.30R0.10R15.00
Tier 2R0.45R0.15R22.50
Tier 3R0.90R0.30R45.00
Tier 4R1.50R0.50R75.00
Tier 5R5.00R1.00R250.00

Use your Standard Bank credit card for every fill — the credit card rates are consistently 3x higher than debit card rates across all tiers. If you are a Standard Bank client, this is the single most actionable fuel decision available.

Double Rewards

UCount Double Fuel Rewards

Available to Standard Bank credit card clients who meet all five qualifying conditions simultaneously — doubles the per-litre earn rate.

Standard Bank UCount offers a Double Fuel Rewards option for credit card clients who meet all qualifying criteria. When active, the per-litre earn doubles across all tiers.

UCount Tier Standard credit (per litre) Double credit (per litre) 50L double earns
Tier 1R0.30R0.60R30.00
Tier 2R0.45R0.90R45.00
Tier 3R0.90R1.80R90.00
Tier 4R1.50R3.00R150.00
Tier 5R5.00R10.00R500.00

Double Fuel Rewards require ALL five qualifying conditions: All spend on a Standard Bank credit card; all fuel purchases at Astron Energy / Caltex stations; active Standard Bank transactional account; two or more additional Standard Bank products; and meeting the monthly qualifying criteria set by UCount. Verify your current eligibility at ucount.standardbank.co.za.

Redemption

What You Can Do With UCount Rewards

UCount Rewards points are versatile — one of the broader redemption options among SA bank loyalty programmes.

  • Pay for fuel directly at Astron Energy / Caltex pumps using your UCount Rewards balance — the most straightforward redemption.
  • Shop at Checkers, Shoprite, and Checkers Sixty60 — use UCount points at the till.
  • Parking and airport lounges — pay for selected parking facilities and airport lounge access.
  • Tax-Free Savings Account (TFSA) — invest UCount Rewards points into a TFSA directly. This is the standout UCount feature unavailable in most other loyalty programmes.
  • Loan repayments — contribute UCount points toward a Standard Bank personal loan repayment.
  • Education donations — donate to qualifying education causes through the UCount programme.

Astron Energy Daily Rewards

Astron Energy runs its own Daily Rewards programme (powered by Payment24) that operates separately from UCount Rewards. Every fill-up enters you into an instant-win draw for fuel vouchers or convenience store vouchers — redeemable on the spot.
