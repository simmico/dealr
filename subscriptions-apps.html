Apps & Services.

Spotify, YouTube Premium, Microsoft 365, and more — covered by SA bank rewards you may already be earning.

FNB eBucks — Spotify Premium

FNB account holders at eBucks Level 4 and above can redeem eBucks towards a Spotify Premium individual subscription each month. At Level 5, the full monthly subscription is covered. Navigate to the eBucks portal under Entertainment > Music > Spotify and link your account. The redemption is processed at the start of each billing cycle.

Tip: If you are on eBucks Level 3, you still receive a partial discount. Boost your level by consolidating your spending through your FNB card and meeting the salary deposit requirement each month.

FNB eBucks — Udemy Courses

The FNB eBucks store lists Udemy course credits and subscriptions as a redemption option. You can redeem eBucks for course credits, effectively getting professional development courses — from coding to design to business skills — at no out-of-pocket cost. Browse the eBucks Lifestyle section for current available Udemy offers.

Tip: Udemy regularly runs promotions where courses drop from R2,000+ to around R200. Stack an eBucks redemption with a Udemy sale to maximise value.

Nedbank Greenbacks — Microsoft 365

Nedbank Greenbacks members can redeem points for Microsoft 365 Personal and Family subscriptions. The Microsoft 365 Personal subscription covers one user with 1TB OneDrive storage and the full Office suite. Browse the Nedbank Money app under Greenbacks > Tech > Microsoft for current redemption rates and availability.

Tip: Microsoft 365 Family covers up to 6 people — split the cost with family members and use Greenbacks to cover your share, making it effectively free for the household organiser.

YouTube Premium via Network Bundles

Several Vodacom and MTN postpaid contract tiers include YouTube Premium as a bundled benefit, providing ad-free YouTube, background play, and YouTube Music. When comparing contracts at upgrade time, look for plans that list YouTube Premium as an included benefit — it removes the separate R109/month fee completely.

Tip: YouTube Premium also includes YouTube Music Premium at no extra charge. If you currently pay for both Spotify and YouTube separately, a contract bundle covering YouTube Premium may be more cost-effective.
