TotalEnergies.

What TotalEnergies Club offers and how to make the most of it if you regularly fill up at TotalEnergies stations.

TotalEnergies Club

How TotalEnergies Club Works

A points-based loyalty programme for fuel and in-store purchases at participating TotalEnergies service stations.

TotalEnergies Club is TotalEnergies' loyalty programme for South African fuel customers. Members earn points on fuel purchases and on qualifying in-store purchases at TotalEnergies Bonjour convenience stores at participating stations.

Earning points

Points are earned per litre of fuel purchased and per rand spent in-store. The earn rate and point conversion is set by TotalEnergies and is subject to change — check the TotalEnergies website or in-store information for current rates applicable at your local station.

Redeeming points

Accumulated points can be redeemed at participating TotalEnergies stations. Redemption options typically include fuel discounts or in-store purchases at the Bonjour convenience shop. The minimum point threshold for redemption and available redemption categories are managed through the TotalEnergies Club portal or app.

Registration

Registration is typically available at participating TotalEnergies stations in South Africa or via the TotalEnergies website. The card or app is required to earn points on each fill-up.

Context

TotalEnergies as Part of Your Fill Strategy

TotalEnergies stations are available across South Africa and the fuel quality is standard. The TotalEnergies Club programme is worth activating if you fill up there regularly — points that accumulate passively over time represent a small but real return on purchases you were already making.

Personally, if maximising your per-litre reward is the priority, stations with more structured loyalty stacking — BP with multiple partner cards, Engen for FNB eBucks clients, or Astron Energy for UCount — offer more significant cashback for the same volume. That said, TotalEnergies Club is worth having active if TotalEnergies is your most convenient option or a regular part of your route.

Not financial advice. This page is informational only. TotalEnergies Club programme terms, earn rates, and redemption options change over time. Check the TotalEnergies website or contact a participating station for current details applicable to your situation.
