Standard Bank UCount.

Tier your rewards higher, fill up at Caltex, and redirect what you earn straight into a tax-free investment - UCount has genuine wealth-building potential at Tier 4.

How UCount tiers work

Standard Bank UCount operates on a four-tier system (Tiers 1 to 4) determined by a combination of your product holdings with Standard Bank and your digital spend behaviour. The more products you hold - transactional account, credit card, home loan, vehicle finance - and the more active your card use, the higher your tier.

The value anchor is: 10 UCount Points = R1.00. At Tier 4, the earn rate on fuel, groceries, and partner spend becomes genuinely competitive with other major SA bank programmes. The tier structure is the key variable - the same spend at Tier 1 earns significantly less than at Tier 4.

Caltex fuel strategy

UCount has a dedicated fuel partnership with Caltex (now trading as Astron Energy) service stations. Filling up exclusively at Caltex using your linked Standard Bank card earns up to R5.00 back in UCount Points per litre at Tier 4.

On a 50-litre fill-up at Tier 4, that is R250 back in points - enough to meaningfully offset a weekly fuel cost over the course of a month. For Standard Bank clients who drive regularly, this single partnership alone can justify the account.

The strategic move is straightforward: pick a Caltex station on your regular route and make it your consistent fill-up point. Consistency at the same partner station is what stacks the points over time.

Grocery and retail stacking

Beyond fuel, UCount has dedicated grocery and lifestyle partner networks. Routing your grocery spend to Pick n Pay and other UCount partner retailers earns up to 20% back in points on qualifying spend at Tier 4.

The principle of partner stacking is the same as with fuel: concentrate your discretionary spend at partner merchants rather than splitting it across multiple retailers. The more consistently you shop at UCount's partner network, the faster your points accumulate and the higher your tier grows.

Directing rewards into investments

This is UCount's most distinctive feature - and the one that separates it from every other major SA bank rewards programme. You can direct your accrued UCount reward balance directly into a Tax-Free Savings Account (TFSA) or a Standard Bank investment structure.

Rather than spending points on vouchers or cashbacks into your cheque account, you are compounding them inside a tax-sheltered investment vehicle. Over time, that difference in trajectory is substantial. For anyone with a long investment horizon, UCount points going into a TFSA are worth considerably more than the same points spent on day-to-day purchases.

If you are already maximising your annual TFSA contribution limit, surplus points can route into other investment pockets instead - still compounding, still growing, still working.

Signature and Infinite concierge access

Standard Bank Signature and Infinite cardholders unlock access to a regional travel planning and lifestyle assistance desk. The service handles restaurant reservations, event ticketing, travel itinerary support, and personal requests - operating as a dedicated assistance resource rather than a generic call centre.

This sits alongside the VISA Signature and Infinite privileges that come with those card tiers - both programmes operate in parallel and independently of one another.
