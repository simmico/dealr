BP Rewards.

SA's most stackable fuel loyalty programme — a single fill can earn you cashback, Smart Shopper points, and Discovery Miles all at once.

Best overall loyalty

How BP Rewards Works

Free to register. Earn cashback on every litre of fuel and every rand spent at bp Express.

Base earn rate

Swipe your BP Rewards card at any participating BP station to earn 20c per litre in cashback on fuel. You also earn 1% cashback on all purchases at bp Express convenience stores. The card is free to register at bprewards.co.za or by scanning the QR code on any BP Rewards physical card at the pump.

Cashback accumulates in your BP Rewards balance. Once your balance reaches R20 or more, you can redeem it at the pump toward your next fill, or spend it at any bp Express store — no minimum purchase required in-store once the R20 threshold is met.

The double-swipe strategy

At the pump you swipe your BP Rewards card and one partner card on the same transaction. Each programme counts independently — they do not cancel each other out. The table below shows what each combination earns:

Your combination Per-litre earn 50-litre fill earns
BP Rewards only 20c/l R10.00 cashback
BP Rewards + PnP Smart Shopper 20c/l + 10 pts/l R10.00 + 500 pts (≈ R5.00)
BP Rewards + Nedbank Greenbacks 45c/l combined (20c + 25c) R22.50 cashback
BP Rewards + Discovery Vitality Drive (Diamond) 20c/l + up to 50% of monthly spend R10.00 + significant monthly cashback

Note: PnP Smart Shopper and Discovery Insure cards cannot be swiped on the same transaction — you choose one partner per fill-up. Nedbank Greenbacks requires an eligible Nedbank card or the BP-linked Nedbank product. All three partner programmes can each be used separately with the BP Rewards base card.

Discovery Insure

Vitality Drive at BP

The most valuable add-on available at BP for Discovery Insure clients — up to 50% of your monthly fuel spend back based on your driving behaviour.

If you have Discovery Insure with Vitality Drive, BP is one of the best places in SA to fill up. Your driving behaviour each month earns Vitality Drive points, and those points determine your cashback percentage on your total monthly BP fuel spend. The better and safer you drive, the more you earn back.

Partner Programme

PnP Smart Shopper at BP

Earn 10 Smart Shopper points per litre of fuel when you swipe your PnP Smart Shopper card at BP — in addition to the BP Rewards 20c/l base. Smart Shopper points are the same points you earn at Pick n Pay tills. They accumulate across all PnP purchases and convert to real rand cashback.

You can also redeem R20 or more in Smart Shopper points toward your fuel payment at participating BP stations linked to the PnP Smart Shopper programme. This means if you shop regularly at PnP, your fuel can effectively become a partial redemption vehicle for in-store points earned elsewhere.

  • 50 litres earns 500 Smart Shopper points — approximately R5.00 in cashback value.
  • Points are redeemable at PnP tills or toward fuel at participating BP stations (minimum R20).
  • Requires a PnP Smart Shopper card linked to a qualifying PnP account.
Partner Programme

Nedbank Greenbacks at BP

Eligible Nedbank clients can earn an additional 25c per litre at BP on top of the standard BP Rewards 20c/l base — bringing the combined earn rate to 45c/l at BP with both cards. This is the highest per-litre fixed cashback rate available at BP without Discovery Insure.

Requires an eligible Nedbank credit or debit card, or the BP-linked Nedbank product. Swipe your Nedbank card when paying at the BP pump alongside your BP Rewards card — both are processed in the same transaction.

  • Combined with BP Rewards: 45c/l total — R22.50 on a 50-litre fill.
  • Greenbacks credit is added to your Nedbank Greenbacks balance for redemption at partner stores.
  • Check greenbacks.nedbank.co.za for current card eligibility.
Programme Rules

Redemption & Account Rules

  • Minimum R20 cashback balance to redeem at the BP pump or bp Express store.
  • Points do not expire while your account is active — defined as at least one earn or redeem transaction in any rolling 3-month window.
  • Inactive accounts: all cashback and points expire after 12 months of zero activity.
  • A virtual BP Rewards card is available in your digital wallet — no physical card required to earn or redeem.
  • You can link or update your card details at any participating BP station or via the BP Rewards website.
