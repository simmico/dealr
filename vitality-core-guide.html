Vitality Status, Explained.

Your status tier is what unlocks everything else. Here's what it costs and how to climb.

What Vitality is and what you are paying for

Discovery Vitality is a science-based behaviour change programme that rewards you for knowing and improving your health. It tracks your health status through screenings and assessments, your physical activity through gym visits and fitness devices, your nutrition through HealthyFood purchases, and your sleep. The more you engage, the higher your Vitality status - and the richer the benefits.

The Vitality Active membership rate is R159 per month per adult. Vitality Health membership is available through Discovery Health medical scheme plans. Your status tier - Blue, Bronze, Silver, Gold, or Diamond - directly controls the depth of your discounts across gym, groceries, travel, wearables, entertainment, and more.

Diamond is the highest tier and requires achieving Gold status two years in a row - it cannot be reached in your first year on the programme. Everything starts from Blue, which is the default starting point for all new members.

How to earn Vitality points

Points reset to zero on 1 January every year. Whatever tier you hold on 31 December carries into the new year - but earning starts from scratch in January. Your target for the year is to accumulate enough points to reach or maintain your desired status tier before December.

For a single adult Vitality Health member, the point thresholds for 2026 are: Bronze - 7,500, Silver - 25,000, Gold - 40,000, Diamond - Gold two years running.

The major point-earning categories and their annual caps are:

  • Know your health (up to 25,000 pts/year): Complete your Vitality Health Check (blood pressure, glucose, cholesterol, weight, smoker status) - worth up to 22,500 points based on results. Add your Vitality Age online assessment (1,500 pts) and Mental Wellbeing Assessment (500 pts x 2 per year). Age-specific screenings like mammograms, Pap smears, colonoscopies, and dental checks each add 1,000-2,500 points.
  • Get active (up to 35,000 pts/year): Gym workouts and approved fitness activities earn up to 100 points each visit. Tracking exercise with a compatible smartwatch or fitness app earns up to 300 points per day. Parkrun completions, padel sessions, HYROX events, and Vitality-timed race events all earn additional points. Your Vitality Cardio Fitness Level (VO2 max) tracked through Apple, Garmin, or Samsung devices adds up to 10,000 points annually.
  • Eat well (up to 12,000 pts/year): Buying HealthyFood items at Checkers and Woolworths earns points based on your Vitality HealthyFood Score. The more qualifying healthy items in your basket, the higher your score and the more points you earn.
  • Sleep well (up to 6,000 pts/year): Track and maintain excellent sleep habits through a compatible sleep tracking device to earn points based on your Vitality Sleep Score.

The fastest routes to Gold

The most efficient single action is completing your Vitality Health Check - done at any accredited Vitality Wellness Centre, participating pharmacy, Discovery Store, or Wellness Day. With results in range, this alone can contribute up to 22,500 points. Combined with the Vitality Age assessment (1,500 pts) and the Mental Wellbeing Assessment (1,000 pts over two completions), your health checks alone can get a single adult close to Silver in one visit.

From there, consistent gym activity (at least 3 sessions per month to keep your gym discount at 75%), HealthyFood spending at Checkers or Woolworths, and VO2 max tracking via a wearable device are the most reliable ongoing point sources. For families, pooling points across all adults on the membership helps - but the threshold also scales up with membership size.

Discovery Vitality's integration with Discovery Bank, Discovery Life, and Discovery Insure means members with multiple Discovery products can unlock boosted rewards through those integrations - worth exploring if you hold or are considering additional Discovery products.

What your status unlocks

The key benefits available across Vitality tiers include: up to 75% off gym membership at Virgin Active or Planet Fitness, up to 25% back in Discovery Miles on HealthyFood at Checkers and Woolworths, half-price Ster-Kinekor movie tickets (50% off 2D, 3D, and Cinema Nouveau; 25% off IMAX and Prestige), a fully-funded Apple Watch Series SE 3 or Series 11 through Active Rewards, up to 25% off local and international flights and car hire through the Discovery Miles travel ecosystem, and weekly rewards through Vitality Active Rewards for achieving exercise goals.

At Diamond level, you also unlock access to a Garmin watch at up to 25% off and up to 8 free gym visits per month through Vitality Fitness. The full benefit set expands year on year - keep checking your Discovery app for newly activated benefits during the year.
